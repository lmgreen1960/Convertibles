The Convertibles

We'll bring the music.

The Convertibles are a 4-piece band formed while in college. Their songlist spans the decades from the 50's through today. They pride themselves on their diversity of sound, covering everything from Journey, to The Cars, to Earth, Wind and Fire. All members sing, adding 4 part harmony to the mix. Their shows are enjoyed at corporate, private, and music hot spots throughout Northern California.
